    The task was to take a lengthy Wikipedia page and compile the content into a book. I chose the page on augmented reality and integrated AR elements to create a more interactive, engaging reading experience. Assets were 3D rendered on Blender and made into filters on Instagram via Meta Spark Studios, interspersed at crucial points of the book to further enhance the content within.
