    This cookbook is defined by and born out of a desire to preserve the Korean dishes that shaped my childhood, and honor my grandmother. Two translations were involved in the making of this cookbook: Korean to English, and culinary measurements that come from a lifetime of cooking for others to standardized measurements so that these dishes are more easily replicable. The typeface used is my own handwriting, inspired by the traditional process of recording recipes on paper. All illustrations are hand drawn, and feature the staples of Korean cooking used in these recipes.
