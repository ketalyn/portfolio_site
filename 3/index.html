    This two-part project references a collection of photos taken on subway rides, documenting various footwear and leg positions during these short-lived encounters. A manifestation of the thought processes that happen when interacting with strangers, DOUBLE TAKE contrasts pieces of writing and imagery that goes surface-deep with ponderings on the serendipitous intertwining of other lives with our own.
